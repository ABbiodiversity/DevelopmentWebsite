Review process

Mammals and birds are reviewed on an opt in basis, meaning that we only present results for species where we are confident in the models due to higher scrutiny for these species. Other groups are opt out, meaning that we do not present results for species where we are not confident.
  1. Organize results on the development website
  2. Identify and notify internal and external reviewers
  3. Conduct review (ClickUp, Google Form)
  4. Finalize species lookup tables
  5. Update dev website and package result for handoff (tabular outputs and maps).
